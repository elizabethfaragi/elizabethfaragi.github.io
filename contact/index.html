Elizabeth Faragi is a budding journalist with a focus in political work. Originally from New Jersey, Elizabeth is pursuing a joint degree in journalism and government and politics at the University of Maryland. Her previous education was at Westfield High School, where she served as editor-in-chief of OPTIC Magazine and as social media manager of Hi's Eye Newspaper. She has previously worked at the Westfield Leader and Union County HAWK, where she learned valuable skills in interviewing, writing, copy editing, photography and organization. Currently, Elizabeth works for The Diamondback as a copy editor since August 2024 and serves as a staff writer at Stories Beneath the Shell since August 2025.

Skills Include: Reporting, Copy Editing, AP Style, Writing, Editing, Photography, Leadership
