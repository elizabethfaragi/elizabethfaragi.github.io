Elizabeth Faragi is a budding journalist with a focus in political work. Originally from New Jersey, Elizabeth is pursuing a joint degree in journalism & government and politics at the University of Maryland. Her previous education was at Westfield High School, where she served as editor-in-chief of OPTIC Magazine and as social media manager of Hi's Eye Newspaper. Currently, Elizabeth serves as a reporting intern at NorthJersey.com, where she works to report and write three stories a week, covering local North Jersey news. She also serves as the General Assignment editor at Stories Beneath the Shell, an independent student publication at the University of Maryland, where she manages freelance reporters and holds weekly general assignment meetings with her co-GA editor. Previously, Elizabeth has worked as an intern at the College Park Here & Now and The Westfield Leader and Union County HAWK, where she wrote mulitple articles, profiling county officials, local business and covering local events. She also formerly worked at The Diamondback, an independent student publication at the University of Maryland, as a copyeditor, where she learned skills in copyediting and AP Style. Elizabeth is focused on accountable reporting and holding those in power responsible while giving voice to communities who cannot speak for themselves. She looks forward to continuing to practice strong and unbiased reportng.
