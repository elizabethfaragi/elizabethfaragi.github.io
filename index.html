Elizabeth Faragi is a budding journalist with a focus in political work. Originally from New Jersey, Elizabeth is pursuing a joint degree in journalism & government and politics at the University of Maryland. Her previous education was at Westfield High School, where she served as editor-in-chief of OPTIC Magazine and as social media manager of Hi's Eye Newspaper. She has previously worked at the Westfield Leader and Union County HAWK, where she learned valuable skills in interviewing, writing, copy editing, photography and organization. Currently, Elizabeth works for The Diamondback, the university's indepedent student newspaper, as a copy editor and serves as a staff writer at Stories Beneath the Shell, another student-run publication. She has worked at The Diamondback since August 2024 and started at Stories Beneath the Shell in August 2025. Elizabeth also works at the College Park Here & Now as of January 2026, where she covers local news and city council. Elizabeth is extremely passionate about informing the public on local issues and current events surronding government. She focuses on delivering unbiased news to allow the public to make informed decisions on their government.
